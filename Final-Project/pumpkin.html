How To Carve a Halloween Pumpkin

- Group of carved Jack-o-Lanterns. Credit: Unsplash

By Hamza Elafifi

Published December 7th, 2021

Halloween is just around the corner and the Fall weather is approaching. A major symbol of the Halloween spirit is the jack-o-lantern. A pumpkin with a scary face to send chills down your spine.

All you need to do is find the right, firm, pumpkin and choose any design you like! Many have gone for the original scary face, while others choose more original approaches of choosing symbols or designs of a range of things- from people, animals, cartoon characters, etc.

From where you start to cut the pumpkin, and how to avoid mistakes like cutting too much, these pro tips will help you bring your Jack-O-Lantern to life!

What You'll Need

  • Carving tool
  • Marker
  • Pumpkin
  • Scraper Scoop
  • Damp Cloth

Step 1. Find a Good Pumpkin

Go to any nearby market to find the right pumpkin. It should have a sturdy stem, no bruises and a flat bottom.

- The Perfect Pumpkin. Image Source: Shutterstock

Step 2. Cut Open the Pumpkin from the Top

Take your pumpkin and use your carving tool to cut open the top. First, cut around the stem at an angle, remove it, and keep it for the later steps.

Step 3. Remove all of the pulp

Take your scraper scoop and remove all of the seeds and pulp inside the pumpkin. Make sure to keep the pulp and seeds if you plan on planting or making pumpkin pie.

- Scrapping out the seeds. Image Source: Image Stock

Step 4. Start your design

When you've thought of your design, take a piece of paper and start sketching. Once you're happy with your design, start to draw it on the pumpkin using your marker.

- Designing the pumpkin. Image Source: Womensday.com

Step 5. Start cutting

Start by making rough cuts on the pumpkinâ€™s design. After you've done the rough cuts, start by cutting them out precisely and make sure to cut directly on the line to avoid cleaning the excess marker.

Step 6. Finishing off

Once the design is cut out and you're happy with your Jack-O-Lantern, take the lid you cut out and place it back on top to finish off your Jack-O-Lantern.

Step 7. (OPTIONAL) Add a candle

Many people add candles inside the pumpkin to give it a spookier feeling. Take a candle and place it inside the pumpkin through the opening in the top. Light the candle and close off the pumpkin with its stem.

You're Done!

There you have it. A Jack-O-Lantern and a lot of happy children! Lots of people prefer to have their Jack-O-Lanterns on their stairway or right beside a window so they are viewable by everyone. However you feel like it, place the Jack-O-Lantern any place you like!
